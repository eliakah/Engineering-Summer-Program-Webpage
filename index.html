Community & K-12 Outreach

Middle and High School Field trips to the Henry M. Rowan College of Engineering can be scheduled by emailing engineering@rowan.edu. An engineering overview, building tour and a hands-on engineering project led by engineering students are typically included in the experience but an engineering itinerary can be customized to your needs, based upon availability. Please note, transportation to and from the Henry M. Rowan College of Engineering and lunch are not provided in this experience.
